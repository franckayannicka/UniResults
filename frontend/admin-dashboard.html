Assign Courses to Lecturers

Link courses to lecturers for a given academic year.

New Assignment
Instructions

1. Select a lecturer from the dropdown.

2. Choose the course to assign.

3. Set the academic year (default: 2024/2025).

4. Click Assign Course.


Once assigned, the lecturer can view the course and upload marks for enrolled students.

Enroll Students

Register students into courses.

New Enrollment
Instructions

1. Select a student from the dropdown.

2. Choose the course to enroll them in.

3. Set the academic year.

4. Click Enroll Student.


Enrolled students appear in the lecturer's course roster and can view their results once marks are uploaded.
